Welcome to Cook&Go! We have made this website to showcase our international backgrounds through food. All three of us are from three different countries: Ireland, Canada and Korea. In today’s fast-paced society, it is sometimes difficult to find time to create satisfying meals and snacks. The recipes introduced in this website are recipes we believe are worthy of sharing because they are not only very quick to make, but also have cultural significance.
